About MIDTEX

MIDTEX is a purely online business dedicated to delivering high-quality household items and appliances directly to customers. Our mission is to provide reliable, affordable, and durable products while maintaining excellent customer service. We aim to simplify shopping through digital convenience and fast WhatsApp ordering.

Our values include integrity, transparency, affordability, and customer satisfaction. We continuously improve our product range to meet modern home needs.

Terms & Conditions

By placing an order with MIDTEX, customers agree to confirm product availability and pricing through WhatsApp before payment. Orders are processed after confirmation. Delivery timelines depend on customer location and product availability.

MIDTEX respects customer privacy. Personal information such as names, phone numbers, and delivery addresses collected during ordering will be used strictly for order processing and communication purposes.
